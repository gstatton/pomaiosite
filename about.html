About POMA

Bechtel established the Project Office for Mobile Applications (POMA) in San Francisco to address the growing demand for mobile applications. POMA officially began operations in September of 2011 at the 50 Beale office. In January 2012, the POMA Team moved into their studio at 217 2nd Street.
